Story

Peter is 23 years old and is still learning how to balance his hero work and his normal life. He has just defeated the “Kingpin” a.k.a Wilson fisk. He volunteers at a homeless shelter named F.E.A.S.T. He works with his Aunt May and their boss, Martin Li. Peter’s actual “normal” job is being a scientist at Octavius Industries led by Otto Octavius. Peter and Mary Jane (MJ) are separate from each other and haven’t talked to each other since. Until MJ was on a secretive mission and almost got caught by Demons. Demons are new type of enemy that Peter has never faced before. But in the process of taking down the Demons and finding their leader, Peter caused the trail of dominos to topple over. The city has faced a bombing by the Demons. Which has caused a group of casualties. (SPOILER ALERT) One of them was officer Jefferson Davis. Who had just received an award for saving Spider-Man’s life and for his service.(Spoiler) And because of that sacrifice. Peter is determined to find and defeat the leader of the Demons. But even Peter’s spidey sense couldn't detect the real danger that’s imminent.

So far there are 2 DLC (downloadable content) out. The first one to come out is “The Heist”,“Turf Wars", and finally "Silver Lining". The series of these packs are called "The City that never Sleeps".

  • The Heist

This DLC pack is all about Peter's past. Black Cat a.k.a Felicia Hardy comes back to Manhattan with a new goal. Peter is worried about what happened in the past. Before the game took place. When MJ and Peter first separated, Peter was dating Felicia. And because of that Peter convinced Felicia to work with him to fight crime. But Felicia decided to break up with Peter and quit being the Black Cat to start a new life in a different city. But now she's back with a new plan to manipulate Peter to do her dirty work with the Maggia gang.

  • Turf Wars

This pack is a continuation to "The Heist". It's about Peter fighting off the Maggia family gang. He has to face off against the stongest forehead in New York. No not the Hulk,nor Iron Man, and not Thor. NO not Thanos, he has the stongest chin in the universe. It's Hammerhead. His name is Hammerhead because he kills people with his forehead. Why does he have the stongest forehead? When Hammerhead was little he severely damaged his skull. So he got a most of his skull replaced with a metal alloy almost like a metal plate. Hammerhead is the head (with the stongest forehead) of the Maggia (one of New York's crime families). He wants to take over New York. But Spider-Man and NYPD are there to stop him. But the captain of NYPD, Yuri Watanabe, has a personal issue with Hammerhead. So far the only the story gives us is that Hammerhead made it personal a long time ago and killed many of Yuri's men. Not only that but he got his hands on Sable International technology. Which is like the government of the this Earth. But Peter and the officers took Hammerhead down or so it would seem.

Web-Swinging

Now this game has brought back a mechanic which has never been used except for "Spider-Man 2." This mechanic is ... PHYSICS!!! This mechanic allows the webs to actually stick to objects. So when you web-swing through the city, you can see that the web strand actually stuck to the building. This is so GAME CHANGING (get it because this is about a game okay I'll show myself out). I know that was a really bad joke but physics change the way this game is played. In other Spider-Man games, the webs usually stick to the sky. Which isn't really realistic. But this mechanic makes it so that you (the player) can't swing above the Empire State Building and makes them actually feel like Spider-Man. Physics make Peter the "friendly neighborhood Spider-Man" he truly is.

Insomniac Games

The developer of this game is Insomniac Games. They are the guys that created the great Ratchet and Clank. They primarily got chosen to create Spider-Man because they created a type of physics that are similar to the on in the original Spider-Man 2. They first started to play around with physics when they created their hit "Sunset Overdrive." Which is really loved for the physics. This is not related but they also made the original Spyro the Dragon game.
